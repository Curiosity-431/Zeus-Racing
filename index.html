Who are we?

A little crew of engineering students who have started working towards their passion. Amidst the normal college life of engineering student which dealt with writing examination and doing assignment presentation,our craze and love towards cars, drove us to form an effective team for making our dream come true.

"A Dream of making a quality Racing Car"


We Zeus racing hereby commence our career by making an electric car for the upcoming Formula-E Racing

Our Vision

1. Bringing out the importance of electric cars and inspiring young students to engage in innovative tasks towards the improvement of technology.
2.Providing a standard platform for young talent and change their path from the scholar to a professional.

Our Mission

1. To build a electric car which will be one best competitors to other cars participating in Formula-E.
2. To unleash the hidden talents of students and apply them towards innovative ideas.
